Inter-School badminton tournament-2019

Venue: Sathish Sivalingam Indoor stadium, Velammal Vidyalaya (Annexure) School

Date: 24th October, 2019

Role: Event Organizer

Links: news

Theesma’s Inter-School badminton competition held at Sathish Sivalingam Indoor stadium, Velammal Vidyalaya (Annexure) School on 24th October 2019 was a grand success with more than 150 participants in 4 categories- Under 10, 13, 15 and 17 singles – boys and girls.

The winners and runners were honoured with cash prize and trophies by the CEO of Theesma Events, Miss Priyanka and all the other participants were awarded certificates.
