நானும் ஒரு விவசாயி (Naanum Ooru Vivasayi)

Venue: Avaniyur - Nallanilam near Dhindivanam

Date: 29th August 2017

Role: Event Organizer- As a Freelancer

Reference: Guinness World Record

Miss Priyanka in partnership with a charitable trust, "Maaruvom Maatruvom" run by actor Aari, organised a Guinness World Record event called "Naanum Oru Vivasayi" on 29th August 2017 at Avaniyur - Nallanilam near Dhindivanam. The record was achieved with 2683 participants by planting 5366 saplings, two each. Students from Sathyabama and J.P.R colleges along with a lot of other people. This team has broken China's record in which 2017 people participated

On the big event, the crowd was cheered by the Tamil folk singer, Chinnaponnu along with Parai dancers. Stunt master Mr Pandian's Silambattam team entertained the crowd.

Dr.Kamal Hassan inaugurated the event with a press meet. Miss Priyanka, Founder of Theesma, along with her team received the Guinness certificate from Actor Aari on the month of November 2018 for their achievement. This record motivated Priyanka to start working on her dream “Theesma.”
